Description

A tropical blend of rich coconut cream, white rum and tangy pineapple, serve with an umbrella for kitsch appeal

    Ingredients
  • 120ml pineapple juice
  • 60ml white rum
  • 60ml coconut cream
  • wedge of pineapple, to garnish (optional)
    Instructions
  1. Pulse all the ingredients along with a handful of ice in a blender until smooth. Pour into a tall glass and garnish as you like.
  2. For a frozen blended version Mix the juice, rum and coconut cream together and freeze in ice cube trays. Then whiz the frozen cubes (with a couple of ice cubes if you like) to a slushy consistency.
